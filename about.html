About ODDSOCEAN

ODDSOCEAN is not just an Esports organization – it is a movement of gamers, dreamers, and creators. We started with a vision to make gaming in India stand at the global stage, and today, we are a name every gamer recognizes.


🔥 Who We Are

➝ A dedicated team of pro players, content creators, and innovators.
➝ Believers in the power of gaming as a career, culture, and lifestyle.
➝ Dreamers who don’t just play games, but also build experiences for the community.


🎮 What We Do

➝ Compete in major Esports titles like BGMI, Free Fire, Scarfall 2.0 & Clash of Clans.
➝ Organize tournaments & events that give a platform to emerging gamers.
➝ Support creators & streamers to grow and reach global audiences.
➝ Build a community-first ecosystem for gamers across India.


🚀 Vision 2030 & Beyond

➝ Establish ODDSOCEAN as India’s own AAA Game Development Studio.
➝ Deliver world-class games that can compete globally.
➝ Expand into merchandise, live events, and global collaborations.
➝ Make ODDSOCEAN a household name in gaming & entertainment.


💎 Our Core Values

  • 🎯 Esports Excellence – Compete, win, and inspire.
  • 🤝 Community First – Gamers are family, always.
  • ⚡ Innovation – From tournaments to games, we push limits.
  • 🌍 Global Impact – Putting Indian gaming on the world map.

📅 Our Journey

2022 – The idea of ODDSOCEAN was born.
2023 – Participated in national-level tournaments & built our first roster.
2024 – Grew into a recognized Esports org & community hub.
2025 – Set our sights on becoming India’s AAA game studio.
